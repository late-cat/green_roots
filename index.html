About

In a world facing rapid urbanization and deforestation, Project Green Roots aims to inspire a culture of environmental responsibility among students and the community. Initiated by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Narula Institute of Technology, this project promotes large-scale tree plantation and awareness about the importance of greenery in maintaining ecological balance. Every tree planted is a step towards cleaner air, cooler surroundings, and a healthier planet for future generations.

on occasion of 78th independence day,the NCC unit of NiT organised a tree plantation drive.
